Mahesh Babu, born Ghattamaneni Mahesh Babu on August 9, 1975, is a prominent Indian actor, producer, media personality, and philanthropist known for his work in Telugu cinema. Here's some detailed content about him: Birth and Family: Mahesh Babu was born in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, to veteran Telugu actor Krishna and Indira Devi. He has four siblings: two brothers, Ramesh Babu and Naresh, and two sisters, Padmavathi and Manjula. Education: Mahesh completed his schooling at St. Bede's Anglo Indian Higher Secondary School in Chennai and obtained a bachelor's degree in commerce from Loyola College, Chennai
